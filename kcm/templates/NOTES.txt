Thank you for installing the kcm Helm chart!

To get started with your deployment:

1. Check the status of your release:
  helm status {{ .Release.Name }}

2. Get the list of pods:
  kubectl get pods -l app.kubernetes.io/instance={{ .Release.Name }}

3. To view logs for the kcm pod:
  kubectl logs -l app.kubernetes.io/instance={{ .Release.Name }}

4. To upgrade this chart:
  helm upgrade {{ .Release.Name }} {{ .Chart.Name }} -f values.yaml

For more information, visit:
https://github.com/your-org/kcm

Happy Helming!
